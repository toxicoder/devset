# Director of Sales

**Role Code:** SALE0002

## Job Description
The Director of Sales leads a large sales region or segment (e.g., Enterprise, Mid-Market), reporting to the VP of Sales. They manage Sales Managers and ensure the team hits their quotas. They are responsible for forecasting, sales strategy execution, and developing sales talent.

## Responsibilities

* **Sales Leadership:** Drive revenue growth and meet quarterly targets.
* **Team Management:** Hire and manage Sales Managers.
* **Forecasting:** Provide accurate revenue forecasts to the VP of Sales.
* **Strategy:** Implement sales methodologies and playbooks.
* **Deal Support:** Assist in closing complex or high-value deals.

### Role Variations

#### The Enterprise Director
Focuses on long sales cycles, high ACV, and relationship building.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 Forecast Review
* 11:00 Client Visit
* 14:00 Deal Strategy
* 16:00 Executive Sponsor Call

#### The SMB/Mid-Market Director
Focuses on high velocity, volume, and process optimization.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 Pipeline Review
* 11:00 Call Coaching
* 13:00 Data Analysis
* 15:00 Sales Ops Sync

## Common Partners
VP of Sales, Director of Marketing, Sales Ops
