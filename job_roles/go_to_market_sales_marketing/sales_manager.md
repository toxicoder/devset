# Sales Manager

**Role Code:** SALE0003

## Job Description
The Sales Manager leads a team of Account Executives (AEs) or SDRs, reporting to a Director of Sales. They are responsible for the daily performance of their team, coaching reps, and ensuring pipeline health.

## Responsibilities

* **Team Management:** Daily coaching, 1:1s, and pipeline reviews with reps.
* **Hiring:** Recruit and train new sales representatives.
* **Performance Management:** Track activity and revenue metrics.
* **Deal Coaching:** Help reps structure deals and negotiate.
* **Motivation:** Keep the team motivated and focused.

### Role Variations

#### The AE Manager
Manages closing roles (Account Executives).
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 08:30 Pipeline Scrub
* 10:00 Deal Review
* 13:00 Interview
* 16:00 Closing Call Support

#### The SDR Manager
Manages prospecting roles (Sales Development Reps).
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 08:30 Standup
* 10:00 Call Listening
* 13:00 List Building Strategy
* 16:00 Activity Review

## Common Partners
Director of Sales, Marketing Manager, Sales Enablement
