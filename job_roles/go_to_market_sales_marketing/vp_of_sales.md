# VP of Sales

**Role Code:** SALE0001

## Job Description
The VP of Sales leads the sales organization, responsible for revenue generation and customer acquisition. They manage the sales team (AEs, SDRs, SEs), set sales strategy, and ensure that targets are met. The VP of Sales is a key driver of the company's growth.

## Responsibilities

* **Revenue Growth:** Hit or exceed quarterly and annual revenue targets.
* **Sales Strategy:** Define sales methodologies, territories, and compensation plans.
* **Team Leadership:** Hire, train, and manage a high-performing sales team.
* **Pipeline Management:** Ensure a healthy sales pipeline and accurate forecasting.
* **Customer Relationships:** Build relationships with key executive stakeholders at customer organizations.

### Role Variations

#### The Process VP
Focuses on the "Sales Machine." Metrics, funnels, MEDDIC, scripts. Everything is standardized.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 Pipeline Review
* 11:00 Training Session
* 14:00 CRM Audit
* 16:00 Forecast Call

#### The Deal-Maker VP
Loves being in the field. Spends 50% of time on planes or Zoom calls with customers.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 Customer Meeting
* 11:00 Negotiation
* 14:00 Closing Call
* 16:00 Strategy Dinner

#### The Channel VP
Focuses on partners and resellers. "The best way to scale is through others."
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 Partner Call
* 11:00 Channel Strategy
* 14:00 Reseller Training
* 16:00 Enablement Review

## Common Partners
CRO, VP Marketing, VP Product, VP Finance
