CLO: Chief Legal Officer

Role Code: EXEC0006

Job Description

The Chief Legal Officer (CLO) is the head of the corporate legal department and is responsible for the legal affairs of the entire corporation. They provide legal counsel to the board of directors, chairman of the board, CEO, and other senior management. The CLO ensures that the company operates within the law at all times, protects the company‚Äôs intellectual property, and manages litigation.

Responsibilities

  • Legal Strategy: Advise executive leadership on legal risks and business strategies.
  • Compliance: Ensure the company complies with all relevant laws and regulations (GDPR, CCPA, SEC, etc.).
  • Contract Management: Oversee the drafting, review, and negotiation of all major contracts.
  • IP Protection: Manage and protect the company‚Äôs intellectual property portfolio (patents, trademarks).
  • Litigation Management: Manage all legal disputes and coordinate with outside counsel.

Involved in business strategy. Uses the law as a competitive advantage (e.g., patent moats, regulatory arbitrage). Average Daily Tasks:

  • 09:00 Exec Strategy Sync
  • 11:00 M&A Deal Structure
  • 13:00 IP Strategy

Common Partners
