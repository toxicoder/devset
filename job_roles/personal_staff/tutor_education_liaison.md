# Tutor / Education Liaison

**Role Code:** PERS0008

## Job Description
Oversees the educational development of the principal or their family. The Tutor provides direct instruction, manages school relationships, and plans enrichment activities.

## Responsibilities

* **Direct Instruction:** Provide tutoring in specific subjects or languages.
* **Curriculum Oversight:** Monitor school progress and coordinate with teachers.
* **Enrichment:** Plan educational outings, reading lists, and cultural experiences.
* **College Counseling:** Assist with university applications and test preparation.
* **Skill Acquisition:** Facilitate learning new skills (e.g., coding, music) for the principal.

### Role Variations

#### Governor / Governess
Focuses on the upbringing, manners, and education of children.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 08:00 School Drop-off
* 10:00 Lesson Planning
* 15:00 Homework Help
* 17:00 Etiquette Lesson

#### Adult Education Coach
Focuses on continuous learning for the principal (languages, history, tech).
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 Language Lesson
* 11:00 Research Brief
* 14:00 Tech Tutorial
* 16:00 Reading Discussion

## Common Partners
Personal Chief of Staff, Household Manager
