# Personal Shopper / Stylist

**Role Code:** PERS0007

## Job Description
Curates the principal's image and lifestyle. The Personal Shopper/Stylist manages the wardrobe, sources gifts, and ensures the principal is impeccably turned out for every occasion.

## Responsibilities

* **Wardrobe Management:** Organize closets, seasonal rotations, and cataloging items.
* **Styling:** Create outfits for daily wear, travel, and special events.
* **Sourcing:** Purchase clothing, accessories, and hard-to-find luxury items.
* **Gifting:** Select and wrap thoughtful gifts for friends, family, and associates.
* **Tailoring:** Coordinate alterations and repairs for garments.

### Role Variations

#### Image Consultant
Advises on personal branding, grooming, and public appearance.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 10:00 Brand Review
* 12:00 Fitting
* 14:00 Media Kit Review
* 16:00 Grooming Appt

#### Gift Concierge
Specializes purely in corporate and personal gifting strategies.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 Holiday List Prep
* 11:00 Sourcing Antiques
* 13:00 Wrapping
* 15:00 Delivery Logistics

## Common Partners
Personal Chief of Staff, Event Coordinator, Travel Concierge
