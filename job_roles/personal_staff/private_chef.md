# Private Chef

**Role Code:** PERS0003

## Job Description
Responsible for all culinary needs of the principal and their family. The Private Chef plans menus, sources high-quality ingredients, and prepares healthy, delicious meals tailored to specific dietary requirements.

## Responsibilities

* **Menu Planning:** Design weekly menus based on preferences and dietary goals.
* **Sourcing:** Shop for organic, high-quality ingredients from specialized vendors.
* **Cooking:** Prepare breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks daily.
* **Kitchen Management:** Maintain kitchen hygiene, organization, and equipment.
* **Event Catering:** Execute menus for dinner parties and social gatherings.

### Role Variations

#### Nutritional Chef
Specializes in specific diets (Keto, Vegan, Paleo) and works closely with trainers.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 07:00 Market Run
* 09:00 Meal Prep
* 12:00 Lunch Service
* 15:00 Macro Calculation

#### Travel Chef
Travels with the principal to maintain dietary consistency on the road.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 08:00 Provisioning in new city
* 11:00 Kitchen Setup
* 13:00 Cooking
* 18:00 Packing for next leg

## Common Partners
Household Manager, Personal Trainer, Event Coordinator
