# Personal Assistant

**Role Code:** PERS0013

## Job Description
The dedicated 'doer' for daily personal tasks. Distinct from the strategic Chief of Staff, the Personal Assistant handles errands, logistics, and ad-hoc requests to ensure the principal's day runs smoothly.

## Responsibilities

* **Errand Running:** Grocery shopping, dry cleaning, pharmacy pickups, and deliveries.
* **Logistics:** Driving the principal, coordinating vehicle maintenance, and route planning.
* **Pet Care:** Dog walking, vet visits, and feeding.
* **Scheduling Support:** Handling personal appointments (salon, dentist) and reminders.
* **Gift Purchasing:** Buying and wrapping small gifts or flowers on demand.

### Role Variations

#### The Body Man/Woman
Travels everywhere with the principal, carrying bags, managing immediate needs, and acting as a buffer.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 07:00 Bag Prep
* 09:00 Travel with Principal
* 12:00 Lunch Pickup
* 18:00 Debrief

#### Family Assistant
Supports the wider family, including driving kids to school and managing family calendars.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 08:00 School Run
* 10:00 Grocery Run
* 14:00 House Org
* 15:30 School Pickup

## Common Partners
Personal Chief of Staff, Household Manager, Nanny/Tutor
