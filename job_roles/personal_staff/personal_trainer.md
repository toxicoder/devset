# Personal Trainer

**Role Code:** PERS0004

## Job Description
Dedicated to the physical health and wellness of the principal. The Personal Trainer designs and supervises workout regimens, monitors progress, and advises on lifestyle choices to ensure peak physical condition.

## Responsibilities

* **Program Design:** Create personalized fitness plans based on goals (strength, endurance, flexibility).
* **Coaching:** Lead daily workout sessions with proper form and motivation.
* **Progress Tracking:** Monitor metrics like weight, body fat, and performance benchmarks.
* **Recovery:** Assist with stretching, foam rolling, and injury prevention.
* **Lifestyle Advice:** Provide guidance on sleep, hydration, and ergonomics.

### Role Variations

#### Wellness Coach
Holistic approach including meditation, stress management, and mental health.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 07:00 Yoga/Meditation
* 09:00 Workout Session
* 11:00 Nutrition Review
* 14:00 Lifestyle Audit

#### Performance Coach
Focuses on athletic performance for specific sports (e.g., Tennis, Triathlon).
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 06:00 Conditioning
* 08:00 Drills
* 10:00 Video Analysis
* 13:00 Strategy Session

## Common Partners
Private Chef, Personal Chief of Staff
