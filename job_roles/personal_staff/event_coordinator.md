# Event Coordinator

**Role Code:** PERS0006

## Job Description
The creative force behind social engagements. The Event Coordinator conceptualizes, plans, and executes private events, ranging from intimate dinner parties to large-scale galas.

## Responsibilities

* **Concept Design:** Develop themes, decor, and atmosphere for events.
* **Vendor Coordination:** Hire and manage florists, musicians, caterers, and rentals.
* **Guest Management:** Manage invitations, RSVPs, and seating charts.
* **On-site Execution:** Oversee the event flow and resolve issues in real-time.
* **Budget Management:** Track event expenses and negotiate contracts.

### Role Variations

#### Social Secretary
Focuses on etiquette, correspondence, and managing the social calendar.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 Correspondence
* 11:00 RSVP Tracking
* 13:00 Gift Registry
* 15:00 Seating Plans

#### Party Planner
Focuses on the creative and production aspects of events.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 10:00 Decor Sourcing
* 12:00 Venue Walkthrough
* 14:00 Tasting Menu
* 16:00 Run of Show

## Common Partners
Private Chef, Household Manager, Personal Shopper
