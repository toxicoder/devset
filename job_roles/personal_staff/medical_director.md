# Medical Director

**Role Code:** PERS0012

## Job Description
The lead physician responsible for the holistic health strategy of the principal. The Medical Director coordinates care, manages specialists, and ensures immediate access to world-class medical treatment.

## Responsibilities

* **Health Strategy:** Develop a long-term longevity and wellness roadmap.
* **Care Coordination:** Vet and manage relationships with specialists (cardio, derm, etc.).
* **Emergency Response:** Maintain 24/7 readiness for medical emergencies, anywhere in the world.
* **Bio-hacking/Longevity:** Research and implement cutting-edge treatments for life extension.
* **Routine Care:** Conduct check-ups, administer vaccinations, and manage prescriptions.

### Role Variations

#### Private Physician
General practitioner available on-call for all day-to-day medical needs.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 08:00 Vitals Review
* 10:00 Patient Visit
* 13:00 Specialist Consult
* 16:00 Research

#### Mental Health Coach
Focuses on psychology, stress management, and cognitive performance.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 Session
* 11:00 Meditation Guide
* 14:00 Sleep Analysis
* 16:00 Cognitive Drills

## Common Partners
Personal Trainer, Private Chef, Personal Chief of Staff
