# VP of Design

**Role Code:** DESN0001

## Job Description
The VP of Design leads the design organization, ensuring that the company's products are beautiful, intuitive, and consistent. They oversee Product Design, UX Research, and Creative/Brand Design. The VP of Design advocates for the user experience at the executive level and establishes the design culture.

## Responsibilities

* **Design Strategy:** Define the design vision and principles for the company.
* **Design System:** Oversee the creation and maintenance of the design system.
* **Team Leadership:** Hire, mentor, and manage designers and researchers.
* **UX Quality:** Ensure a high bar for visual and interaction design quality.
* **Brand Alignment:** Ensure product design aligns with the company's brand identity.

### Role Variations

#### The Visionary VP
Focuses on the "North Star" design. Pushes boundaries and challenges the status quo.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 Inspiration Session
* 11:00 Design Critique
* 14:00 Future Concepts Review
* 16:00 Brand Strategy

#### The Systems Thinker
Focuses on scalability, design tokens, and efficiency. Builds the "Lego blocks" of the product.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 Design System Review
* 11:00 Tooling Sync
* 14:00 Component Library Audit
* 16:00 Doc Review

#### The Research-Led VP
Starts everything with user data. Heavily invested in UX Research and usability testing.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 User Interview Review
* 11:00 Research Synthesis
* 14:00 Persona Workshop
* 16:00 Usability Lab

## Common Partners
CPO, VP Product, VP Engineering, VP Marketing
