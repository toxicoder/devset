# Director of Finance

**Role Code:** FINC0002

## Job Description
The Director of Finance leads a major financial function (e.g., FP&A, Accounting, Strategic Finance), reporting to the VP of Finance. They manage managers and analysts and are responsible for accurate financial reporting, forecasting, and strategic advice.

## Responsibilities

* **Financial Strategy:** Drive financial planning and analysis for the company.
* **Team Leadership:** Manage the finance team.
* **Reporting:** Oversee the preparation of financial statements and board materials.
* **Compliance:** Ensure adherence to financial regulations and standards.
* **Partnership:** Act as a financial partner to department heads.

### Role Variations

#### The FP&A Director
Focuses on budgeting, forecasting, and modeling.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 Forecast Model
* 11:00 Dept Head Sync
* 14:00 Variance Analysis
* 16:00 Board Deck Prep

#### The Accounting Director
Focuses on the close process, audit, and tax.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 GL Review
* 11:00 Audit Mtg
* 13:00 Tax Strategy
* 15:00 Controls Review

## Common Partners
VP of Finance, Controller, Dept Heads
