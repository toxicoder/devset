# CPO: Chief Product Officer

**Role Code:** EXEC0003

## Job Description
The Chief Product Officer (CPO) is responsible for the strategic direction of the product portfolio. They lead the product management, design, and user research teams, ensuring the company builds products that customers love and that drive business value. The CPO aligns product strategy with the overall company vision and oversees the entire product lifecycle from ideation to launch.

## Responsibilities

* **Product Strategy:** Define the product vision, strategy, and roadmap in alignment with company goals.
* **Portfolio Management:** Manage the portfolio of products, making decisions on investment, sunsetting, and innovation.
* **User Advocacy:** Champion the user's voice at the executive level, ensuring a customer-centric approach.
* **Cross-Functional Alignment:** Collaborate with Engineering, Sales, and Marketing to ensure successful product launches.
* **Team Leadership:** Recruit, mentor, and lead a world-class product and design organization.

### Role Variations

#### The Visionary CPO
Focuses on the "next big thing." deeply intuitive about market trends and user behavior. Often drives radical innovation.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 10:00 Concept Review
* 13:00 Design Critique
* 15:00 Market Trend Analysis
* 17:00 Strategy Deck

#### The Analytical CPO
Data-driven. Obsessed with metrics, conversion rates, and A/B testing. Believes the truth is in the numbers.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 Metrics Review
* 11:00 Growth Hacking Sync
* 14:00 Data Science Review
* 16:00 Revenue Modeling

#### The Operational CPO
Focuses on execution and process. optimizing how product, design, and engineering work together.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 Roadmap Sync
* 11:00 Process Optimization
* 13:00 Resource Planning
* 15:00 Launch Readiness Review

## Common Partners
CEO, CTO, CRO, VP Product
