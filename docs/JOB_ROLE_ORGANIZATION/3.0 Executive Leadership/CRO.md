# CRO: Chief Revenue Officer

**Role Code:** EXEC0004

## Job Description
The Chief Revenue Officer (CRO) oversees all revenue-generating processes in the organization. This includes Sales, Marketing, and Customer Success. The CRO is responsible for ensuring that all revenue streams are aligned and optimized for growth. They own the go-to-market strategy and are accountable for top-line revenue performance.

## Responsibilities

* **Revenue Strategy:** Develop and execute a comprehensive revenue strategy to drive growth.
* **GTM Alignment:** Align Sales, Marketing, and Customer Success to ensure a seamless customer journey.
* **Sales Leadership:** Oversee the sales organization, setting quotas, compensation plans, and territories.
* **Forecasting:** Provide accurate revenue forecasts to the CEO and Board.
* **Partnerships:** Identify and cultivate strategic partnerships that drive revenue.

### Role Variations

#### The Closer CRO
A super-salesperson who leads from the front. Often gets involved in closing the biggest deals personally.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 Deal Review
* 11:00 Client Lunch
* 14:00 Negotiation Call
* 16:00 Pipeline Review

#### The System Builder CRO
Focuses on the "Revenue Engine." Obsessed with funnel metrics, conversion rates, and sales operations efficiency.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 Dashboard Review
* 11:00 Ops Sync
* 13:00 Comp Plan Design
* 15:00 Marketing Alignment

#### The Growth CRO
Background in Marketing or Product. Focuses on product-led growth (PLG) and high-velocity sales models rather than enterprise sales.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 Lead Gen Review
* 11:00 Pricing Strategy
* 14:00 User Journey Map
* 16:00 Experiment Review

## Common Partners
CEO, CFO, CPO, VP Sales, VP Marketing
