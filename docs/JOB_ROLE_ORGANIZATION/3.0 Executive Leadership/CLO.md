# CLO: Chief Legal Officer

**Role Code:** EXEC0006

## Job Description
The Chief Legal Officer (CLO) is the head of the corporate legal department and is responsible for the legal affairs of the entire corporation. They provide legal counsel to the board of directors, chairman of the board, CEO, and other senior management. The CLO ensures that the company operates within the law at all times, protects the company's intellectual property, and manages litigation.

## Responsibilities

* **Legal Strategy:** Advise executive leadership on legal risks and business strategies.
* **Compliance:** Ensure the company complies with all relevant laws and regulations (GDPR, CCPA, SEC, etc.).
* **Contract Management:** Oversee the drafting, review, and negotiation of all major contracts.
* **IP Protection:** Manage and protect the company's intellectual property portfolio (patents, trademarks).
* **Litigation Management:** Manage all legal disputes and coordinate with outside counsel.

### Role Variations

#### The General Counsel
Traditional legal role. Focuses on contracts, risk, and compliance. Very thorough and risk-averse.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 Contract Review
* 11:00 Board Meeting Prep
* 14:00 Policy Update
* 16:00 Outside Counsel Call

#### The Strategic CLO
Involved in business strategy. Uses the law as a competitive advantage (e.g., patent moats, regulatory arbitrage).
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 Exec Strategy Sync
* 11:00 M&A Deal Structure
* 13:00 IP Strategy
* 15:00 Lobbying Update

#### The Compliance Specialist
Heavily focused on regulatory environments (FinTech, HealthTech). Ensures the company doesn't get shut down by regulators.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 Regulatory Audit
* 11:00 Compliance Training
* 14:00 Data Privacy Review
* 16:00 Risk Assessment

## Common Partners
CEO, CFO, VP People, Corp Counsel
