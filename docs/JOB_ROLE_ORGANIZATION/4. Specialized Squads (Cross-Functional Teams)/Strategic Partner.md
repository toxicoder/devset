# Strategic Partner

## Purpose
Integrations with major tech partners.

## Responsibilities

### API Integration & Certification
*   Design and build robust integrations with partner APIs (e.g., Salesforce, Slack, Microsoft).
*   Manage the authentication and authorization flows (OAuth, SSO) for secure connectivity.
*   Navigate partner app store certification processes and ensure compliance with their technical requirements.
*   Monitor API changes and deprecations to maintain uninterrupted service.

### Relationship Management
*   Serve as the technical point of contact for external partners during integration and troubleshooting.
*   Collaborate with business development to identify and prioritize high-value integration opportunities.
*   Provide documentation and support to partners building on top of our platform.
*   Participate in joint roadmap planning sessions to align feature releases.

### Legal & Security Compliance
*   Review partner data sharing agreements and ensure technical implementation adheres to terms.
*   Conduct security assessments of partner integrations to prevent data leakage.
*   Implement data minimization strategies to only share necessary information.
*   Ensure compliance with regional data residency requirements when exchanging data with partners.
