# Event Strategy

## Purpose
Plans major annual conferences.

## Responsibilities

### Logistics Planning & Operations
*   Manage end-to-end logistics for major conferences, including venue selection, vendor management, and travel.
*   Coordinate onsite operations, registration, and attendee flow to ensure a seamless experience.
*   Manage the event budget, tracking expenses and negotiating contracts to maximize ROI.
*   Develop contingency plans for potential disruptions (e.g., technical failures, health safety).

### Brand Activation & Content
*   Design the visual identity and physical branding for the event space (booths, signage, stage).
*   Curate the agenda, selecting speakers and managing content development for keynotes and sessions.
*   Coordinate with marketing to promote the event and drive registration.
*   Create engaging experiences and swag to reinforce brand values.

### Attendee Experience & Engagement
*   Map the attendee journey to ensure every touchpoint is positive and impactful.
*   Implement event technology (apps, networking tools) to facilitate connection and learning.
*   Gather and analyze attendee feedback to measure satisfaction and improve future events.
*   Manage community engagement and social media presence during the event.
