# Sustainability (ESG)

## Purpose
Tracks carbon footprint and green initiatives.

## Responsibilities

### Carbon Tracking & Reporting
*   Measure and report on the organization's Scope 1, 2, and 3 greenhouse gas emissions.
*   Implement software tools to automate carbon accounting and data collection.
*   Produce annual sustainability reports aligned with standards like SASB or GRI.
*   Verify environmental data through third-party audits.

### Green Initiatives & Operations
*   Identify and execute projects to reduce energy consumption in offices and data centers.
*   Procure renewable energy credits (RECs) or establish power purchase agreements (PPAs).
*   Promote sustainable procurement practices and supply chain engagement.
*   Manage waste reduction and recycling programs across all facilities.

### Strategy & Stakeholder Engagement
*   Develop long-term sustainability goals (e.g., Net Zero) and roadmaps.
*   Engage with employees through green teams and sustainability challenges.
*   Communicate ESG progress to investors, customers, and the public.
*   Partner with product teams to design more energy-efficient software.
