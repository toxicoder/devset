# Acquisition (SEO)

## Purpose
Optimizing website/landing pages for organic traffic.

## Responsibilities

### Technical SEO & Performance
*   Audit website architecture and code to ensure crawlability and indexability by search engines.
*   Optimize core web vitals (LCP, FID, CLS) to improve page load speed and user experience.
*   Implement structured data (schema markup) to enhance search result listings.
*   Monitor and fix crawl errors, broken links, and redirect chains.

### Content Optimization & Strategy
*   Conduct keyword research to identify high-value search terms and user intent.
*   Collaborate with content creators to optimize on-page elements (titles, meta descriptions, headers).
*   Develop content clusters and internal linking strategies to build topical authority.
*   Analyze content gaps and recommend new pages to capture incremental traffic.

### Analytics & Experimentation
*   Track and report on organic traffic, rankings, and conversion metrics using tools like Google Search Console and Analytics.
*   Run A/B tests on landing pages to improve click-through rates (CTR) and conversion rates.
*   Monitor competitor SEO strategies and performance to identify opportunities and threats.
*   Attribute organic traffic to business outcomes and refine strategies based on ROI.
