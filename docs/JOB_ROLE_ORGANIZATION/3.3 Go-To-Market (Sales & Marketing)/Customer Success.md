# Customer Success

**Role Code:** CSM9004

## Job Description
A client-centric role dedicated to ensuring customers realize the full value of the product. The Customer Success Manager (CSM) acts as a trusted advisor, guiding customers through onboarding, adoption, and renewal phases. They monitor account health, identify upselling opportunities, and advocate for customer needs within the product organization. This role is key to reducing churn and fostering long-term customer loyalty.

## Responsibilities

* Onboarding;
* Renewals/Upsells;
* Usage monitoring;
* QBRs

## Average Daily Tasks
* 09:00 Email triage
* 11:00 Training
* 14:00 QBR prep

## Common Partners
Account Exec, Support
