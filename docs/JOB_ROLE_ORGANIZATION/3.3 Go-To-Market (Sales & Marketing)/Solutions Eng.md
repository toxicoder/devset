# Solutions Eng

**Role Code:** SALE9003

## Job Description
A technical advisor who bridges the gap between sales and engineering. The Solutions Engineer partners with Account Executives to deliver compelling product demonstrations and technical proof-of-concepts (POCs). They answer complex technical questions, address security and compliance concerns, and remove technical barriers to closing deals. This role requires a deep understanding of the product architecture and the ability to explain it to both technical and non-technical audiences.

## Responsibilities

* **Technical Discovery & Consulting:** Partner with Account Executives to uncover prospect business challenges and technical requirements, mapping them to the capabilities of the platform.
* **Custom Demonstration Delivery:** Configure and deliver tailored product demonstrations that highlight specific features and value propositions relevant to the prospect's needs.
* **Proof of Concept (POC) Management:** Design, build, and manage successful Proof of Concepts, ensuring defined success criteria are met and technical objections are resolved.
* **RFP/RFI Response:** Lead the technical response to Requests for Proposals (RFPs) and Requests for Information (RFIs), ensuring accuracy and completeness of technical answers.
* **Technical Objection Handling:** Serve as the technical authority during sales meetings, addressing questions regarding architecture, security, integrations, and deployment.
* **Sales Enablement:** Create technical assets, white papers, and training materials to enable the broader sales team to understand and position technical features effectively.

### Role Variations

* **Pre-Sales Engineer:** The standard role focused on new business acquisition. Heavily involved in discovery, demos, and POCs to win the deal.
* **Post-Sales Engineer (Implementation Engineer):** Focuses on the technical aspects of onboarding and deployment after the deal is signed. Ensures the solution is integrated correctly.
* **Channel Solutions Engineer:** Supports partner organizations (resellers, SIs) in selling the solution. Focuses on training and enabling partner technical teams.
* **Specialist Solutions Engineer:** Deeply specialized in a specific product area or technology vertical (e.g., Security SE, Cloud Infrastructure SE) and brought in for complex deals.
