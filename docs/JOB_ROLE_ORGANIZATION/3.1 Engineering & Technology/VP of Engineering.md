# VP of Engineering

**Role Code:** SWEN0001

## Job Description
The Vice President of Engineering leads the engineering organization, reporting to the CTO. They are responsible for the execution, delivery, and quality of the software. The VP of Engineering focuses on building high-performing teams, establishing engineering culture, and ensuring that engineering processes are efficient and scalable.

## Responsibilities

* **Engineering Delivery:** Ensure software is delivered on time and meets quality standards.
* **Team Building:** Hire, retain, and develop top engineering talent.
* **Culture:** Foster a culture of technical excellence, collaboration, and psychological safety.
* **Process Improvement:** Optimize the software development lifecycle (SDLC) for velocity and reliability.
* **Budget Management:** Manage the engineering budget, including headcount and infrastructure costs.

### Role Variations

#### The Scale-Up VP
Specializes in taking a team from 20 to 200 engineers. Focuses on organizational structure, career ladders, and hiring pipelines.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 Recruiting Sync
* 11:00 Org Design Planning
* 14:00 Engineering All-Hands
* 16:00 Manager 1:1s

#### The Technical VP
Deeply involved in architectural decisions. Still reviews code occasionally. Respected for technical chops.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 10:00 Architecture Review
* 13:00 Incident Review
* 15:00 Tech Radar Session
* 17:00 Reading PRs

#### The Product-Minded VP
Works very closely with Product Management. Focuses on the "what" and "why" as much as the "how."
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 Product Roadmap Sync
* 11:00 Sprint Demo
* 14:00 Customer Call
* 16:00 Delivery Metrics Review

## Common Partners
CTO, VP Product, Directors of Eng
