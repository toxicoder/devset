# VP of Marketing

**Role Code:** MKTG0001

## Job Description
The VP of Marketing leads the marketing organization, responsible for brand awareness, lead generation, and product positioning. They oversee Product Marketing, Demand Gen, Content, and Brand/Comms. The VP of Marketing ensures the company's story is told effectively and that the sales team has the leads they need to win.

## Responsibilities

* **Brand Strategy:** Build and protect the company brand.
* **Demand Generation:** Drive qualified leads (MQLs) to the sales team.
* **Product Marketing:** Define messaging, positioning, and go-to-market strategy for products.
* **Content Strategy:** Oversee the creation of high-quality content (blog, whitepapers, video).
* **PR & Comms:** Manage external communications and media relations.

### Role Variations

#### The Demand Gen VP
Focused on metrics, paid acquisition, and SEO. "Marketing is a math problem."
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 Ad Spend Review
* 11:00 Lead Quality Sync
* 14:00 Campaign Optimization
* 16:00 Conversion Analysis

#### The Brand VP
Focuses on storytelling, design, and emotional connection. "Marketing is art."
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 Creative Review
* 11:00 Brand Strategy
* 14:00 PR Briefing
* 16:00 Agency Management

#### The Product Marketing VP
Deeply understands the product and the customer. Focuses on enablement and positioning.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 Launch Plan
* 11:00 Sales Enablement
* 14:00 Competitive Analysis
* 16:00 Messaging Workshop

## Common Partners
CRO, VP Sales, VP Product, VP Design
