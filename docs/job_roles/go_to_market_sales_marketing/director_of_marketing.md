# Director of Marketing

**Role Code:** MKTG0002

## Job Description
The Director of Marketing leads a major function within marketing (e.g., Demand Gen, Product Marketing, Brand), reporting to the VP of Marketing. They manage Marketing Managers and are responsible for strategy execution and campaign performance.

## Responsibilities

* **Strategy:** Develop and execute marketing strategies for their function.
* **Team Management:** Hire and lead a team of marketers.
* **Budget Management:** Allocate and optimize marketing spend.
* **Performance Analysis:** Track and report on KPIs (leads, MQLs, brand sentiment).
* **Collaboration:** Work with Sales and Product leadership.

### Role Variations

#### The Demand Gen Director
Focuses on leads, pipeline, and attribution.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 Ad Performance Review
* 11:00 Lead Gen Strategy
* 13:00 Sales Sync
* 15:00 Webinar Planning

#### The Product Marketing Director
Focuses on positioning, messaging, and launches.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 Launch Plan
* 11:00 Messaging Workshop
* 13:00 Content Review
* 15:00 Analyst Briefing

## Common Partners
VP of Marketing, Director of Sales, Director of Product
