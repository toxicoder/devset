# Marketing Manager

**Role Code:** MKTG0004

## Job Description
The Marketing Manager executes marketing campaigns and initiatives, reporting to a Director of Marketing. They may manage junior marketers or contractors. They are responsible for the day-to-day operation of marketing programs.

## Responsibilities

* **Campaign Execution:** Launch and manage marketing campaigns (email, ads, events).
* **Content Creation:** Write or oversee the creation of marketing assets.
* **Vendor Management:** Manage agencies and freelancers.
* **Reporting:** Track campaign performance and report to leadership.
* **Project Management:** Ensure projects stay on track and within budget.

### Role Variations

#### The Content Manager
Focuses on blog posts, whitepapers, and SEO.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 Writing
* 11:00 Editorial Calendar
* 14:00 SEO Review
* 16:00 Social Scheduling

#### The Event Manager
Focuses on trade shows and webinars.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 Vendor Calls
* 11:00 Logistics Planning
* 14:00 Booth Design
* 16:00 Budget Tracker

## Common Partners
Director of Marketing, Sales Manager, Designer
