# Brand Marketing

**Role Code:** MKTG9003

## Job Description
A creative and strategic guardian of the company's brand identity. The Brand Marketing Manager develops and executes comprehensive marketing campaigns that build brand awareness and equity. They oversee the creation of compelling content, manage external agency relationships, and ensure consistent messaging across all channels. This role involves analyzing market trends to position the brand effectively and connecting emotionally with the target audience.

## Responsibilities

* **Brand Strategy & Positioning:** Develop and maintain the company's brand architecture, positioning, and voice, ensuring consistency across all touchpoints and markets.
* **Integrated Campaign Management:** Conceptualize, plan, and execute multi-channel marketing campaigns that drive brand awareness, engagement, and customer acquisition.
* **Media Planning & Buying:** Oversee media strategy and budget allocation, managing relationships with media agencies and partners to optimize reach and impact.
* **Content & Creative Oversight:** Direct the creation of high-quality visual and written content, ensuring all assets align with brand guidelines and resonate with target audiences.
* **Market Research & Insights:** Analyze market trends, consumer behavior, and competitive landscape to inform brand strategy and identify new opportunities for growth.
* **Agency & Vendor Management:** Manage relationships with creative agencies, PR firms, and other external vendors, ensuring high-quality deliverables and efficient resource utilization.

### Role Variations

* **Digital Brand Manager:** Focuses specifically on building brand presence in digital channels, including social media, website, and online advertising. Requires expertise in digital analytics and content marketing.
* **Product Brand Manager:** Aligns brand strategy with specific product lines. Works closely with product marketing to launch new features and ensure product messaging reinforces the overall brand promise.
* **Employer Brand Manager:** Dedicated to building the company's reputation as an employer of choice. Collaborates with HR to attract top talent through recruitment marketing and internal culture initiatives.
* **Global Brand Manager:** Oversees brand strategy across multiple international markets, ensuring global consistency while adapting messaging to local cultural nuances.

## Average Daily Tasks
* 10:00 Agency sync
* 11:00 Creative review
* 14:00 Media plan
