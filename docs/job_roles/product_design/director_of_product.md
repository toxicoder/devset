# Director of Product

**Role Code:** PROD0002

## Job Description
The Director of Product leads a major product line or portfolio, reporting to the VP of Product. They manage a team of Product Managers and Group Product Managers. They are responsible for the strategic direction of their product area, ensuring alignment with company goals, and mentoring the product team.

## Responsibilities

* **Product Strategy:** Define and communicate the product vision and strategy for their portfolio.
* **Team Leadership:** Hire, manage, and mentor Product Managers and Group PMs.
* **Portfolio Management:** Oversee multiple product squads and ensure cohesive execution.
* **Stakeholder Management:** Align with Engineering, Design, and GTM leaders on priorities.
* **Market Intelligence:** Understand market trends, competition, and customer needs.

### Role Variations

#### The Growth Director
Focuses on acquisition, retention, and monetization metrics. Data-driven and experimental.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 Metrics Review
* 11:00 Experiment Ideation
* 13:00 Marketing Sync
* 15:00 Growth Strategy

#### The Platform Director
Focuses on internal tools, APIs, and infrastructure products. Technical and systems-oriented.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 API Strategy
* 11:00 Developer Experience Sync
* 13:00 Architecture Review
* 15:00 Internal Customer Interviews

#### The Innovation Director
Focuses on new zero-to-one products. Entrepreneurial and visionary.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 Market Research
* 11:00 Concept Validation
* 13:00 Prototype Review
* 15:00 Pitch Deck Prep

## Common Partners
VP of Product, Director of Engineering, Director of Design
