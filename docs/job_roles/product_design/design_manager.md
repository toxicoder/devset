# Design Manager

**Role Code:** DESN0003

## Job Description
The Design Manager manages a team of Product Designers, reporting to a Director of Design. They focus on the growth and happiness of their designers, ensuring they have the context and support to do their best work. They also oversee the quality of design output for their specific squad or area.

## Responsibilities

* **People Management:** 1:1s, performance reviews, and career development for designers.
* **Design Critiques:** Facilitate feedback sessions to improve design quality.
* **Resourcing:** Assign designers to projects and manage workload.
* **Hiring:** Recruit and onboard new designers.
* **Collaboration:** Partner with Engineering Managers and Product Managers.

### Role Variations

#### The Craft Manager
Still spends time designing. Leads by example.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:30 Design Time
* 11:00 1:1 with Designer
* 14:00 Critique
* 16:00 UI Polish

#### The Ops Manager
Focuses on process, tools, and efficiency.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 Jira/Linear Triage
* 11:00 Design System Audit
* 13:00 Hiring Sync
* 15:00 Process Doc

## Common Partners
Director of Design, Engineering Manager, Group Product Manager
