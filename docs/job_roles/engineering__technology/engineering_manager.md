# Engineering Manager

**Role Code:** SWEN0005

## Job Description
The Engineering Manager (EM) leads a squad or a team of engineers, reporting to a Director of Engineering. They are responsible for the people management, delivery, and technical health of their team. The EM focuses on unblocking engineers, facilitating career growth, and ensuring the team delivers value consistent with the roadmap.

## Responsibilities

* **People Management:** Conduct 1:1s, performance reviews, and career planning for direct reports.
* **Delivery Management:** Collaborate with Product Managers to plan sprints, estimate work, and track progress.
* **Technical Guidance:** Ensure the team follows engineering standards, though they may not code daily.
* **Hiring:** Interview candidates and onboard new team members.
* **Process Health:** Run agile ceremonies (standups, retrospectives) and improve team workflows.

### Role Variations

#### The Tech Lead Manager (TLM)
A hybrid role for smaller teams. Spends 30-50% of time coding or designing architecture.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:30 Code Review
* 11:00 1:1 with Junior Eng
* 13:00 Architecture Design
* 15:00 Sprint Planning

#### The Team Builder
Focuses on rapid hiring and onboarding for a newly formed squad.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 Sourcing Candidates
* 11:00 Interview Loop
* 14:00 Onboarding Session
* 16:00 Team Bonding Event

#### The Delivery Manager
Focuses heavily on execution, metrics, and cross-team dependencies.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:15 Standup
* 10:30 Roadmap Sync with PM
* 13:00 Blocker Removal
* 15:00 Retrospective

## Common Partners
Product Manager, Product Designer, Director of Engineering
