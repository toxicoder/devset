CPO: Chief Product Officer

Role Code: EXEC0003

Job Description

The Chief Product Officer (CPO) is responsible for the strategic direction of the product portfolio. They lead the product management, design, and user research teams, ensuring the company builds products that customers love and that drive business value. The CPO aligns product strategy with the overall company vision and oversees the entire product lifecycle from ideation to launch.

Responsibilities

  • Product Strategy: Define the product vision, strategy, and roadmap in alignment with company goals.
  • Portfolio Management: Manage the portfolio of products, making decisions on investment, sunsetting, and innovation.
  • User Advocacy: Champion the user‚Äôs voice at the executive level, ensuring a customer-centric approach.
  • Cross-Functional Alignment: Collaborate with Engineering, Sales, and Marketing to ensure successful product launches.
  • Team Leadership: Recruit, mentor, and lead a world-class product and design organization.

  • 15:00 Launch Readiness Review
