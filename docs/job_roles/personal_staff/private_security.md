# Private Security Detail

**Role Code:** PERS0009

## Job Description
Ensures the safety, privacy, and peace of mind of the principal. The Private Security Detail assesses risks, provides close protection, and secures physical and digital assets.

## Responsibilities

* **Risk Assessment:** Evaluate threats to the principal's safety and reputation.
* **Close Protection:** Accompany the principal to ensure physical safety in public.
* **Surveillance:** Monitor security systems at residences and offices.
* **Logistics Security:** Advance travel routes and vet locations/venues.
* **Cybersecurity:** Protect digital devices and communications from intrusion.

### Role Variations

#### Bodyguard
Focuses primarily on physical presence and immediate protection.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 08:00 Route Recon
* 10:00 Escort Duty
* 14:00 Venue Sweep
* 18:00 Perimeter Check

#### Security Director
Strategic role managing a team and overall security architecture.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 Intel Briefing
* 11:00 System Audit
* 13:00 Team Training
* 15:00 Vendor Meeting

## Common Partners
Personal Chief of Staff, Travel Concierge, Household Manager
