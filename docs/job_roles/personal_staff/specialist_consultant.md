# Specialist Consultant

**Role Code:** PERS0014

## Job Description
A high-level expert engaged for deep domain expertise in niche luxury assets or interests. Whether it's cars, art, wine, or aviation, the Specialist Consultant manages the acquisition, care, and curation of the collection.

## Responsibilities

* **Acquisition & Sales:** Source rare items, negotiate purchases, and manage auctions/sales.
* **Collection Management:** Catalog inventory, oversee storage/maintenance, and insure assets.
* **Advisory:** Educate the principal on market trends, provenance, and investment value.
* **Logistics:** Manage the transport and display of assets (e.g., shipping art, transporting cars).
* **Networking:** Connect the principal with other collectors and exclusive events.

### Role Variations

#### Automotive Consultant (Car Expert)
Manages a fleet of luxury/vintage cars. Handles maintenance, track days, and restoration projects.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 Shop Visit
* 11:00 Auction Monitoring
* 14:00 Maintenance Log
* 16:00 Drive Prep

#### Art Curator
Manages a private art collection. Handles framing, hanging, insurance, and gallery relationships.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 10:00 Gallery Visit
* 13:00 Cataloging
* 15:00 Conservation Check
* 17:00 Auction Strategy

#### Sommelier / Wine Consultant
Manages the cellar. Purchases futures, tracks drinking windows, and pairs wines for events.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 10:00 Inventory Check
* 12:00 Tasting
* 15:00 Sourcing
* 18:00 Decanting

## Common Partners
Family Office Director, Household Manager, Personal Chief of Staff
