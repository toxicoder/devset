# Household Manager

**Role Code:** PERS0002

## Job Description
Responsible for the smooth operation of the principal's residence(s). The Household Manager oversees maintenance, staff rosters, inventory, and vendor relationships to ensure a 5-star living experience.

## Responsibilities

* **Property Maintenance:** Schedule and oversee repairs, landscaping, and preventative maintenance.
* **Staff Management:** Create rosters and supervise housekeepers, groundskeepers, and other domestic staff.
* **Inventory Control:** Manage supplies, groceries, and household budgets.
* **Vendor Management:** Source and supervise contractors for renovations or services.
* **Event Support:** Assist in the logistical execution of home-based events.

### Role Variations

#### House Manager / Butler
Hands-on role that includes service duties like serving meals and greeting guests.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 08:00 Breakfast Service
* 10:00 Staff Briefing
* 14:00 Vendor Supervision
* 19:00 Dinner Service

#### Multi-Property Manager
Oversees operations across several properties, often remotely or with travel.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 Video Sync with NY Staff
* 11:00 Maintenance Audit
* 13:00 Budget Reconciliation
* 15:00 Travel to Estate

## Common Partners
Private Chef, Personal Chief of Staff, Event Coordinator
