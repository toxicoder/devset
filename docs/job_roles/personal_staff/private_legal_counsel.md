# Private Legal Counsel

**Role Code:** PERS0011

## Job Description
The dedicated legal guardian for the principal's personal and business interests. The Private Legal Counsel handles contracts, disputes, privacy protection, and reputation management.

## Responsibilities

* **Contract Review:** Review and negotiate contracts for real estate, staffing, and purchases.
* **Reputation Management:** Handle defamation issues, cease & desists, and privacy breaches.
* **Risk Mitigation:** Identify and neutralize potential legal liabilities in personal activities.
* **Entity Management:** Maintain LLCs, trusts, and other legal structures.
* **Dispute Resolution:** Manage litigation and settlements to keep matters out of the public eye.

### Role Variations

#### Entertainment Lawyer
Specializes in talent contracts, royalties, and IP protection for high-profile creatives.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 10:00 Contract Redlines
* 12:00 Negotiation Call
* 15:00 IP Audit
* 17:00 Union Rep Sync

#### Trust & Estate Counsel
Deep specialist in succession planning, wills, and inter-generational transfer.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 Trust Drafting
* 11:00 Client Signing
* 14:00 Tax Law Review
* 16:00 Beneficiary Mtg

## Common Partners
Family Office Director, Personal Chief of Staff, Private Security
