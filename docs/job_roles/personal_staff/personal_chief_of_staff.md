# Personal Chief of Staff

**Role Code:** PERS0001

## Job Description
The strategic head of the personal staff, serving as the principal's right hand. The Chief of Staff manages the principal's schedule, communications, and strategic priorities, ensuring seamless operations across all aspects of personal and professional life.

## Responsibilities

* **Calendar Management:** Oversee complex scheduling, prioritizing time for high-value activities.
* **Staff Oversight:** Manage and coordinate the wider personal staff team.
* **Strategic Triage:** Filter communications and requests to protect the principal's focus.
* **Project Management:** Lead special projects and personal initiatives from conception to completion.
* **Stakeholder Relations:** Act as a gatekeeper and liaison for external contacts.

### Role Variations

#### Executive Assistant
Focuses heavily on administrative execution, travel, and scheduling rather than broad strategic oversight.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 08:00 Inbox Triage
* 09:30 Schedule Review
* 11:00 Travel Booking
* 14:00 Correspondence

#### Estate Manager
Combines Chief of Staff duties with heavy property management responsibilities for multiple residences.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 Staff Meeting
* 11:00 Property Walkthrough
* 13:00 Vendor Calls
* 15:00 Budget Review

## Common Partners
Household Manager, Travel Concierge, Private Security
