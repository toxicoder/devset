# Family Office Director

**Role Code:** PERS0010

## Job Description
The financial architect for the principal's private wealth. The Family Office Director oversees investment strategies, tax planning, estate management, and philanthropic giving, ensuring long-term generational wealth preservation.

## Responsibilities

* **Investment Strategy:** Oversee portfolio allocation, asset management, and due diligence on private deals.
* **Tax & Estate Planning:** Coordinate with external counsel to optimize tax efficiency and structure trusts.
* **Cash Flow Management:** Manage liquidity for lifestyle expenses, capital calls, and large purchases.
* **Risk Management:** Oversee insurance portfolios (property, art, kidnap & ransom) and asset protection.
* **Philanthropy:** Manage charitable foundations, grant-making, and impact investing.

### Role Variations

#### Personal Accountant
Focuses on the day-to-day bookkeeping, bill paying, and financial reporting.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 Bill Pay
* 11:00 Reconciliation
* 14:00 Payroll
* 16:00 Expense Reports

#### Investment Officer
Focuses purely on market analysis, deal flow, and portfolio performance.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 08:00 Market Open
* 10:00 Manager Calls
* 13:00 Deal Memo
* 15:00 Portfolio Review

## Common Partners
Personal Chief of Staff, Private Legal Counsel, Specialist Consultant
