# VP of Finance

**Role Code:** FINC0001

## Job Description
The VP of Finance leads the finance department, reporting to the CFO. They are responsible for the day-to-day financial operations, including accounting, FP&A, tax, and treasury. The VP of Finance ensures the company's financial health and compliance.

## Responsibilities

* **Financial Operations:** Oversee accounting, payroll, AP/AR.
* **Budgeting:** Manage the annual budgeting process.
* **Reporting:** Ensure timely and accurate financial reporting (Monthly Close).
* **Compliance:** Ensure compliance with GAAP, tax laws, and audit requirements.
* **Financial Modeling:** Support strategic decisions with financial analysis.

### Role Variations

#### The Controller VP
Background in accounting (CPA). Focused on accuracy, controls, and audits.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 General Ledger Review
* 11:00 Audit Sync
* 14:00 Month-End Close
* 16:00 Tax Filing

#### The Strategic VP
Background in banking or consulting. Focused on models, growth, and unit economics.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 LTV/CAC Analysis
* 11:00 Board Deck Prep
* 14:00 Pricing Strategy
* 16:00 M&A Model

#### The Operational VP
Focuses on systems and efficiency. Implementing NetSuite, automating payroll, etc.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 ERP Implementation
* 11:00 Process Improvement
* 14:00 Vendor Management
* 16:00 Procurement Policy

## Common Partners
CFO, VP Sales, VP Engineering, VP People
