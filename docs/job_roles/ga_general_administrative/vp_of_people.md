# VP of People

**Role Code:** PEOP0001

## Job Description
The VP of People (or VP of HR) leads the people function, responsible for the employee lifecycle. This includes recruiting, onboarding, performance management, compensation, L&D, and culture. The VP of People ensures that the company is a great place to work and attracts top talent.

## Responsibilities

* **Talent Acquisition:** Oversee recruiting strategy to hit hiring goals.
* **Employee Experience:** Ensure a positive and inclusive work environment.
* **Performance Management:** Run performance reviews and compensation cycles.
* **HR Operations:** Manage benefits, payroll (with Finance), and compliance.
* **Culture:** Champion the company values and diversity, equity, and inclusion (DEI).

### Role Variations

#### The Culture VP
Focuses on engagement, morale, and values. "Culture eats strategy for breakfast."
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 All-Hands Prep
* 11:00 DEI Committee
* 14:00 Employee Survey Review
* 16:00 Culture Event

#### The Talent VP
Background in recruiting. Obsessed with headcount, pipeline, and employer brand.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 Recruiting Sync
* 11:00 Interview Training
* 14:00 Headcount Planning
* 16:00 Offer Approval

#### The Ops VP
Focuses on compensation, benefits, and HR systems.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 Comp Review
* 11:00 Benefits Renewal
* 14:00 HRIS Implementation
* 16:00 Compliance Audit

## Common Partners
CEO, CLO, VP Finance, All VPs
