# Director of People

**Role Code:** PEOP0002

## Job Description
The Director of People (or Director of HR) leads the HR function for a business unit or a specific center of excellence (e.g., Talent Acquisition, People Ops), reporting to the VP of People. They manage HRBPs and Recruiters.

## Responsibilities

* **People Strategy:** Align HR initiatives with business goals.
* **Team Management:** Lead the HR/Recruiting team.
* **Talent Acquisition:** Oversee hiring strategies.
* **Employee Relations:** Handle complex employee issues.
* **Culture:** Champion the company culture and values.

### Role Variations

#### The Talent Director
Focuses on recruiting and employer branding.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 Pipeline Review
* 11:00 Interview Training
* 14:00 Headcount Planning
* 16:00 Closing Candidates

#### The People Ops Director
Focuses on benefits, payroll, and systems.
**Average Daily Tasks:**
* 09:00 Benefits Review
* 11:00 Compliance Audit
* 13:00 Comp Analysis
* 15:00 HRIS Optimization

## Common Partners
VP of People, Legal Counsel, Hiring Managers
