# Product Thinking

## Course Overview
**Course Title:** Product Thinking
**Level:** Intermediate
**Format:** Workshop & Role-playing

## Learning Objectives
*   Master the art of Product Thinking.
*   Build stronger relationships with your team.
*   Navigate complex interpersonal situations.

## Core Curriculum
## User Empathy
* **Walk in their shoes**: Use the product daily ("Dogfooding").
* **Listen to users**: Participate in user research or support calls.

## Problem vs. Solution
* Fall in love with the problem, not the solution.
* Ask "Why?" five times to get to the root cause.

## Metrics that Matter
* **North Star Metric**: The single metric that best captures the core value your product delivers.
* **Vanity Metrics**: Numbers that look good but don't correlate to success (e.g., total downloads vs. active users).

## MVP (Minimum Viable Product)
* Build the smallest thing that delivers value and allows us to learn.
* It's not "half-baked"â€”it's a slice of the cake.

## Practical Exercises
1.  **Role Play:** Pair up with a partner and simulate a scenario related to Product Thinking.
2.  **Reflection:** Write a short journal entry about a past experience.
3.  **Feedback:** Request specific feedback from your manager this week.

## Assessment
*   Participation in the workshop.
*   Self-assessment survey.
