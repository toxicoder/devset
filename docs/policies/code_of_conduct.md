# Code of Conduct

## Purpose
The Company Code of Conduct sets the standard for how we work together to develop and deliver our products, how we protect the company's value, and how we work with customers, suppliers, and others.

## Scope
This policy applies to all employees, contractors, consultants, and interns.

## Core Values
1.  **Integrity**: We are honest and ethical in all our business dealings.
2.  **Respect**: We treat everyone with dignity and respect.
3.  **Innovation**: We strive for excellence and innovation in everything we do.
4.  **Collaboration**: We work together to achieve common goals.

## Guidelines
*   **Conflicts of Interest**: Avoid situations where your personal interests conflict with the company's interests.
*   **Harassment and Discrimination**: We have zero tolerance for harassment or discrimination based on race, color, religion, gender, sexual orientation, etc.
*   **Confidentiality**: Protect confidential information of the company, its customers, and its partners.
*   **Company Assets**: Use company assets, including equipment and intellectual property, for legitimate business purposes only.

## Reporting Violations
If you witness or suspect a violation of this Code, you are encouraged to report it to your manager or HR.

## Disciplinary Action
Violations of this Code may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment.
