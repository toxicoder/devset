# Performance Review Policy

## Purpose
This policy establishes a consistent process for evaluating employee performance and development.

## Cycle
Performance reviews are conducted twice a year:
1.  **Mid-Year Review**: July
2.  **Annual Review**: January

## Process
1.  **Self-Assessment**: Employees complete a self-evaluation of their achievements and goals.
2.  **Peer Feedback**: Employees request feedback from colleagues they have worked with.
3.  **Manager Assessment**: Managers evaluate the employee's performance based on goals, competencies, and feedback.
4.  **Calibration**: Managers meet to ensure consistency and fairness in ratings.
5.  **Performance Discussion**: Manager and employee meet to discuss the review and set goals for the next period.

## Ratings
Performance is rated on a 5-point scale:
1.  Does Not Meet Expectations
2.  Partially Meets Expectations
3.  Meets Expectations
4.  Exceeds Expectations
5.  Outstanding
