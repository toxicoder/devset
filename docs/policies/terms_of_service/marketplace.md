The purpose of this policy is to establish a framework for compliance and integrity.

Sellers are responsible for fulfilling orders promptly and in accordance with the terms listed in their listings.

Buyers agree to pay for Items purchased through the Marketplace. Payment is due at the time of purchase.

### 4.2 Refunds and Returns
Buyers are subject to the Seller's return policy as stated in the listing, as well as our Marketplace Refund Policy.

We charge Sellers a transaction fee for each sale made through the Marketplace. Current fees are listed on our Fees page.
