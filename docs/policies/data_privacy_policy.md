# Data Privacy Policy

## Purpose
This policy ensures that the Company complies with applicable data protection laws and respects the privacy rights of individuals.

## Scope
This policy applies to all personal data collected, processed, or stored by the Company.

## Principles
1.  **Lawfulness, Fairness, and Transparency**: Data is processed lawfully, fairly, and in a transparent manner.
2.  **Purpose Limitation**: Data is collected for specified, explicit, and legitimate purposes.
3.  **Data Minimization**: Data is adequate, relevant, and limited to what is necessary.
4.  **Accuracy**: Data is accurate and kept up to date.
5.  **Storage Limitation**: Data is kept for no longer than is necessary.
6.  **Integrity and Confidentiality**: Data is processed in a manner that ensures appropriate security.

## Data Subject Rights
Individuals have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, and object to processing of their personal data.

## Data Breaches
All data breaches must be reported to the Security Team immediately.
