# Travel and Expense Policy

## Purpose
This policy outlines the guidelines for business travel and the reimbursement of business-related expenses.

## Travel Booking
*   All business travel must be approved by the employee's manager.
*   Travel should be booked through the Company's preferred travel agency.
*   Economy class is the standard for air travel.

## Reimbursable Expenses
*   **Accommodation**: Reasonable hotel expenses.
*   **Meals**: Daily allowance (per diem) for meals.
*   **Transportation**: Taxis, rideshares, or public transportation for business purposes.

## Non-Reimbursable Expenses
*   Personal entertainment (movies, gym fees).
*   Traffic fines.
*   Travel insurance (covered by Company policy).

## Reporting
Expense reports must be submitted within 30 days of the expense being incurred, accompanied by receipts.
