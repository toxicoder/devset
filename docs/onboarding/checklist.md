# Onboarding Checklists

## Welcome!
Welcome to the team! This guide covers **Onboarding Checklists** as part of your onboarding journey.

## Key Information
## Onboarding Checklists

Stay organized with these checklists designed to guide you through your first
month.

### Pre-Boarding (Before Day 1)

* [ ] **Sign Offer Letter**: Ensure all paperwork is signed and returned.
* [ ] **Review Welcome Email**: Check your personal email for details on your
  start date, time, and location.
* [ ] **Laptop Delivery**: Confirm tracking for your laptop delivery (if
  remote).
* [ ] **Complete Background Check**: If applicable, ensure this is in progress.

### Day 1: Welcome

* [ ] **IT Setup**:
  * [ ] Log in to your laptop.
  * [ ] Connect to the VPN/Office WiFi.
  * [ ] Set up your email and calendar.
  * [ ] Install Slack and join #general and #introductions.
* [ ] **HR Orientation**: Attend the scheduled orientation session.
* [ ] **Meet Your Manager**: Have a 1:1 welcome chat with your manager.
* [ ] **Meet Your Buddy**: Connect with your assigned onboarding buddy.
* [ ] **Security Training**: Complete the mandatory security awareness
  training.

### Week 1: Settling In

* [ ] **Team Intros**: Schedule coffee chats (virtual or in-person) with your
  immediate teammates.
* [ ] **Dev Environment**:
  * [ ] Follow the [Engineering Bootcamp](engineering_bootcamp.md) guide (if
    engineering).
  * [ ] Request access to necessary tools via
    [Tools and Access](tools_and_access.md).
* [ ] **Read Up**:
  * [ ] Review [Culture and Values](culture_and_values.md).
  * [ ] Read the [Employee Handbook](../policies/code_of_conduct.md).
  * [ ] Review relevant [Style Guides](../style_guides/).
* [ ] **First Team Meeting**: Attend your team's standup or weekly sync.

### Month 1: Ramping Up

* [ ] **Goals**: Set your 30-60-90 day goals with your manager.
* [ ] **First Project**: Complete a small "starter task" or bug fix.
* [ ] **Deep Dive**: Schedule deep-dive sessions on the architecture or
  products you'll be working on.
* [ ] **Feedback**: Have a check-in with your manager to discuss how onboarding
  is going.
* [ ] **Benefits**: Ensure you've enrolled in health benefits and retirement
  plans.

### 30-60-90 Day Plan Template

#### Days 1-30: Learning & Connecting

* Complete onboarding checklist.
* Setup dev environment.
* Meet 100% of the team.
* Ship first code change.

#### Days 31-60: Contributing & Building

* Pick up larger tasks.
* Participate in code reviews.
* Understand the core architecture.
* Present a demo or learning.

#### Days 61-90: Leading & Independence

* Lead a small feature or initiative.
* Propose improvements.
* Operate independently on daily tasks.
* Mentor a new hire (optional).
